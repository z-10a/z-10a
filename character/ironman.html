Ironman

Tony Stark

  • Name: Tony Stark
  • Date of Birth: 29 May 1970

Iron Man is a fictional superhero character in the Marvel Comics universe. He was created by writer Stan Lee, developed by artist Don Heck and designed by artist Jack Kirby. The character made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963.The character's alter ego is Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who builds a high-tech powered suit of armor to become the superhero Iron Man. Stark's suit is equipped with a variety of technological gadgets and weapons, making him a formidable opponent against his enemies. Iron Man has been featured in numerous comic book series, as well as in a number of television shows, movies, and video games. He is a founding member of the Avengers, a team of superheroes that includes Captain America, Thor, and the Hulk, among others. The character has become a cultural icon and is considered one of the most popular superheroes of all time. Robert Downey Jr. played the character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, which began with the film Iron Man in 2008.
