Phase 1

Marvel Phase 1 refers to the first set of Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films released by Marvel Studios between 2008 and 2012. Here is the list of the movies that make up Marvel Phase 1 in chronological order: Iron Man (2008) The Incredible Hulk (2008) Iron Man 2 (2010) Thor (2011) Captain America: The First Avenger (2011) Marvel's The Avengers (2012) These movies introduced many of the major characters in the MCU, such as Iron Man (Robert Downey Jr.), the Hulk (Mark Ruffalo), Thor (Chris Hemsworth), and Captain America (Chris Evans), as well as the team of heroes known as the Avengers. Marvel's The Avengers brought all of these characters together in a single film and set the stage for the next phase of the MCU. Phase One introduces core characters such as Ironman, Captain America, Hulk, Black Widow, Hawkeye, Nick Fury, War Machine and Loki. It also introduces organisations like S.H.I.E.L.D and Hydra and ancient relics called infinity stones. This is the start of the Infinity Saga.
