Ironman 1

The plot of Iron Man (2008) revolves around the character of Tony Stark (played by Robert Downey Jr.), a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who develops a powerful suit of armor to fight evil forces and protect the world. The story begins with Stark visiting Afghanistan to present his company's latest weapon to the US military. While there, he is kidnapped by a terrorist group known as the Ten Rings, who demand that he build them a weapon of mass destruction. Instead, Stark secretly builds a suit of armor and uses it to escape. Back in the US, he decides to perfect the suit and use it to fight evil forces. With the help of his loyal assistant Pepper Potts (Gwyneth Paltrow) and his friend Rhodey (Terrence Howard), Stark refines the suit and takes on the identity of "Iron Man." However, Stark soon discovers that his own company, run by his business partner Obadiah Stane (Jeff Bridges), has been selling weapons to terrorist groups. Stane attempts to kill Stark and steal the Iron Man technology, leading to a final battle between the two armored suits. In the end, Stark emerges victorious and decides to publicly reveal his identity as Iron Man, setting the stage for future adventures as a superhero.
