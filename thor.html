Thor

Thor Odinson

  • Name: Thor Odinson
  • Date of Birth: 964 A.D

Thor is a fictional superhero character in the Marvel Comics universe. He was created by writer Stan Lee, developed by artist Jack Kirby, and first appeared in Journey into Mystery #83 in 1962. The character is based on the Norse god of thunder and possesses superhuman strength, endurance, and longevity. Thor wields a magical hammer named Mjolnir, which can only be lifted by someone who is worthy. He is also capable of controlling the weather and can summon lightning bolts at will. Thor has been featured in numerous comic book series, as well as in a number of television shows, movies, and video games. He is a founding member of the Avengers, a team of superheroes that includes Iron Man, Captain America, and the Hulk, among others. The character has become a cultural icon and is considered one of the most powerful and popular superheroes in the Marvel Comics universe. Chris Hemsworth played the character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, which began with the film Thor in 2011.
