Black Widow

Natasha Romanov

  • Name: Natasha Romanov
  • Date of Birth: December 3, 1984

Black Widow is a fictional character in the Marvel Comics universe. Her real name is Natasha Romanoff, and she is a highly skilled spy and assassin. She was trained in the Red Room, a secret Soviet program that trained young girls to become skilled agents, and later defected to the United States and joined S.H.I.E.L.D. Black Widow is known for her expertise in hand-to-hand combat, espionage, and martial arts, as well as her use of various weapons such as handguns, knives, and tasers. She has also been a member of the Avengers, a team of superheroes that includes Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, and others. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Black Widow is portrayed by actress Scarlett Johansson and has appeared in several films, including Iron Man 2, The Avengers, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, and Avengers: Endgame. She also has her own standalone film, titled Black Widow, which was released in 2021.
